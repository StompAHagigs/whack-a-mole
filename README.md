# Whack a mole idea

Player moves around playing area, attempting to whack moles. Player has the ability to dash a short distance, then waits for cooldown before being able to dash again

Player may get different weapons at certain points. Starts with classic "hammer".

Moles are static at point of appearing. It may be possible to see a burrowing trail before they appear. Player can use this information as a visual cue.

At some point, some burrow trails chase the player. Moles from these might have weapons. Weapons can be stolen upon defeat?

weapon ideas

* hammer (1 hit to whack)
* dodgeball - can bounce on wall, 1 hit
* whip - longer range, needs 2 hits to whack
* gun - 1 hit, recoil
* cannon - appears on field, run to ti to use?
* random teleport? (collectible item to activate)
